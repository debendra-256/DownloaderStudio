Frequently asked questions

  • What's included in the free online screen recorder?

    The free online screen recorder makes it easy to capture and share your screen with a few clicks. You don’t need an account to record your screen.

    With the free recorder, you can:

    • Record a single browser tab, a single window, or your full screen
    • Record with or without a webcam
    • Record with or without audio
    • Zoom in and out while recording
    • Draw on your screen while recording
    • Celebrate moments with fun screen effects
    • Share with a link or download to your device as an mp4
  • Is the online screen recorder secure and private?

    Yes, the online screen recorder is 100% private and secure. You do not need an account to record your screen and webcam, and your recordings are all stored in browser memory until you choose to save them to your device.

    You also have the option to share your videos on ScreenPal.com, which allows you to share with a secure, customizable link. You can set your link to be unlisted so only people with the link can view your video, or you can add password protection. You can also choose to make your link public for anyone to watch.

  • Is there a watermark on the free online screen recorder?

    No, there are no watermarks on any recordings with the free online screen recorder. You can record with confidence and know that you will be sharing a high-quality, professional-looking video.

  • Which browsers are supported?

    The free online screen recorder works on desktops and laptops in Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Safari, and Firefox. We recommend Chrome or Edge for the best online recording results.

  • Is there a recording time limit?

    There is no strict time limit with the free online screen recorder. The only limits are based on memory and the size of your screen recorder. For unlimited recordings with no time limits, try ScreenPal’s premium desktop screen recorder.

  • How do I share my screen recording?

    You can download your screen recording to your device for free as an MP4 file and share it anywhere. (Screen recordings on Firefox will download as a WEBM file).

    You can also share your video with a secure link on ScreenPal.com for free. Click the “Share on ScreenPal.com” button, then login or sign up for your free account to share with a link.

  • How do I record my screen?

    Recording your screen with the free online recorder is easy.

    • Click the “Start recorder” button – no signup or account is required.
    • Choose whether you want to record a single browser tab, window, or full screen.
    • Click the “Camera” button to include your webcam over your screen.
    • Click the microphone icon to Mute or Unmute your voice narration.
    • Press the red Record button to start your screen capture.
    • While recording, you can highlight important information by zooming in and out or drawing on your screen. You can also celebrate key moments with the effects button.
    • Click “Done Recording” to stop and share.

    You can customize your video title, click “Save Video” to download it to your device, or “Share” on ScreenPal.com to share it securely with a link.

  • How do I record my webcam or camera?

    Click the “Camera” button to record your webcam with the free online screen recorder. You can click and drag to position your webcam anywhere over your screen and turn it on and off during your recording.

    If you want to record only your webcam, you can resize the webcam before you start recording by clicking and dragging the corners to fill the entire recording area.

  • How can I record my screen with audio?

    You can easily record voice narration with the free online screen recorder. Click the microphone icon to turn your audio on (“Unmute”) or off (“Mute”) before you start recording.

  • How do I screen record on Mac and Windows?

    ScreenPal offers three easy ways to record your screen on Windows and Mac. 

    Good:  With the free online screen recorder, you can make quick and easy screencasts from your browser with no watermark and nothing to install.

    Better:  With ScreenPal’s free Chrome extension, you can record in your browser from wherever you are, as well as quick-launch ScreenPal’s desktop creation tools.

    Best:  For a more robust recording experience, download ScreenPal’s desktop screen recorder for Windows and Mac. You can record any area of your screen with or without a webcam. You can record audio from any microphone, internal or external, and capture system audio as a premium feature. You can record up to 15 minutes at a time for free and record unlimited videos. You can remove or blur your background or replace it with a virtual background. You can upload your own virtual background for free, choose from a selection of free backgrounds, or choose from millions of stock backgrounds as a premium feature.

    ScreenPal’s desktop screen recorder offers integrated video editing, allowing you to easily trim and cut your recordings, edit or hide your webcam and cursor, and add rich text, music, transitions, and overlays.
